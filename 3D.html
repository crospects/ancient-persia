This bronze sculpture of a persian princes is just one example of the Persians' beautiful art. Many more bronze sculptures have been found at the sight of the royal center in Persepolis.

This Persian war ship is one of the many used by the Persians durring battle. An example of this is when the Persians tried to invade the Spartans in greece. They sailed many such warships towards the enemy, but were defeated by the faster Spartan ships.
