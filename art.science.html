Art/Science

The Persians made many discoveries that greatly impacted the ancient world. The Persian’s ground breaking work on medicine greatly improved life in their empire. The Persians created the world’s first teaching hospital, and wrote many manuscripts on detecting and healing many different ailments. The ancient Persian scientist Abu Ali Al-Hassan Ibn Al-Haytham discovered the secret of the lens, making it possible to make glasses and microscopes. He also discovered that light is not emitted by the eye and bounced back, but rather reflected off objects that we see. Jabir ibn Hayyan created many of the basic chemical methods like distillation that chemists still use today. The Persians greatly improved the ancient world with their discoveries in physics, chemistry, and biology.
