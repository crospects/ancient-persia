Medicine
Inventions/Discoveries

The persians had an amazing healthcare and hospital system. Some experts even say that "To a very large extent, credit for the entire hostpital system must be given to the persians".

Qanat
Food/Farming

Most of higland Persia is very dry. To get enough water to grow crops, the Persians created a system of underground water delivery called a qanat. Qanats could stretch for up to 100 miles.

Architecture
Art/Science

The Persians were great architects. The royal pallaces at Persepolis and Susa are an example of the amazing buildings created by the Persians.

Cuneiform
Language/Writing

The Persians used a Cuneiform writing system that used triangular wedges for letters. They created a pony express system of mail delivery along the Royal Road.
