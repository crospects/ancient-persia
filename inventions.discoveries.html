Inventions/Discoveries

Healthcare is important and despite popular belief, ancient persia had one of the best health care systems around.

For example, ancient writings on the cures of headaches have been found today, and deemed medically accurate. And ancient manuscripts describe the use of anesthetics during surgery.

The first teaching hospital in the ancient world was in persia, some experts even say that “to a very large extent, the credit for the whole hospital system must be given to Persia”.

Persia’s healthcare system much improved the lives of its ancient inhabitants, even today many people would much benefit from a better healthcare system.
